# Custom Business Software

Kamran is an independent, solo freelance software developer based in Pakistan,
working with clients locally and internationally. He designs and builds
web-based business software from scratch, fitted to a client's exact
workflow rather than requiring the business to adapt to generic
off-the-shelf tooling.

## Stack

ASP.NET Core, .NET MVC, C#, SQL Server, Next.js (for the frontend where the
project calls for one).

## Typical work

Customer portals, booking platforms, and multi-user internal business
applications — replacing spreadsheet-driven or manual processes with a
single system of record.

## Engagement

Fixed-scope project, typically 4–10 weeks, priced upfront based on agreed
scope. No per-user SaaS licensing.
