## Dental Clinic Management System

- Industry: Healthcare
- Problem: Manual patient management with paper records and phone-based
  appointment scheduling led to double bookings, lost patient history, and
  billing errors.
- Solution: Complete clinic management system with digital patient records,
  calendar-based appointment scheduling with conflict detection, automated
  billing tied to procedures, and treatment history tracking.
- Result: Clinic administration time reduced significantly; double bookings
  eliminated; patient history instantly accessible during consultations.
- Stack: .NET MVC, SQL Server, C#, JavaScript, Bootstrap.
- Full case study: https://www.kamrandeveloper.com/case-studies/dental-clinic-management

## School Management System

- Industry: Education
- Problem: Manual academic administration — paper attendance,
  spreadsheet-based fee tracking, no parent communication tools.
- Solution: Centralized platform covering enrollment, daily attendance,
  grades and exams, fee plans with automated overdue alerts, timetables,
  and a parent portal.
- Result: 600+ students managed; teachers save roughly 2 hours/week; fee
  collection rate improved through automated reminders.
- Stack: .NET MVC, Web API, SQL Server, C#, JavaScript.
- Full case study: https://www.kamrandeveloper.com/case-studies/school-management

## Retail & Inventory Software

- Industry: Retail
- Problem: Stock discrepancies between system and physical counts, purchase
  orders tracked in spreadsheets, no real-time multi-location stock
  visibility.
- Solution: Multi-location inventory system with location-aware stock
  tracking, full purchase-order lifecycle management, and automated
  reorder suggestions.
- Result: Stock discrepancies dropped from roughly 12% to under 1.5%;
  overstock reduced by about 20% through better visibility.
- Stack: .NET MVC, MySQL, C#, JavaScript, Bootstrap.
- Full case study: https://www.kamrandeveloper.com/case-studies/retail-inventory

## Related: POS System (retail operations software)

Not a "management system" in the healthcare/education sense, but the same
architectural approach applied to retail point-of-sale: real-time inventory
updates, barcode scanning, offline mode with sync, and end-of-day
reconciliation reporting. 60% reduction in end-of-day reconciliation time;
inventory discrepancies dropped from 15% to under 2%.

- Full case study: https://www.kamrandeveloper.com/case-studies/pos-system
