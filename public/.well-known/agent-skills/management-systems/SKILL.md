# Management Systems

Kamran builds complete management platforms for healthcare, education and
retail — patient records, appointment scheduling, billing, attendance, and
inventory — engineered around the specific rules of each industry rather
than a generic template.

## Stack

.NET MVC, ASP.NET Core Web API, C#, SQL Server.

## Delivered examples

See `references/case-studies.md` for full detail on:

- Dental clinic management system (patient records, appointment calendar
  with conflict detection, billing, treatment notes)
- School management system (enrollment, attendance, grades, fee management,
  parent portal)
- Retail & inventory management system (multi-location stock tracking,
  purchase orders, supplier management)

## Engagement

Fixed-scope project, typically 6–14 weeks, delivered and tested module by
module before full integration.
