🚀 One-Click Cross-Poster for karmandeveloper.com

Paste your article title + URL once, copy platform-specific post text, hit "Open all" to launch every publishing window in parallel.

How to use this: (1) Replace title/URL if you're posting something different. (2) Edit any platform's text — it remembers your changes. (3) "Copy" puts the platform-specific post on your clipboard. (4) "Open" launches the publishing page in a new tab. (5) "Open all" launches them all (you'll have ~15 tabs — paste and publish into each).
